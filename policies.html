Refund Policy

Last Updated: October 16, 2025

14-Day Money-Back Guarantee

We offer a full refund if you're not satisfied with your purchase.

No questions asked. If our product doesn't meet your needs within 14 days of purchase, we'll refund your payment in full.

Eligibility

You can request a refund if:

  • You request within 14 days of purchase
  • You haven't violated our Terms of Service
  • You provide order details for verification

How to Request

Email support@f823llc.com with:

  • Subject: "Refund Request"
  • Your order number or license key
  • Purchase email address
  • Brief reason (optional but appreciated)

Processing Time

  • Acknowledgment: Within 24 hours
  • Refund processed: Within 5-7 business days
  • Money in account: 5-10 business days (bank dependent)

After Refund

Once your refund is processed:

  • Your license is terminated
  • Repository access is revoked
  • You must delete all copies of the software
  • License keys become invalid
