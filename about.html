About F823 LLC

We build tools that help developers and entrepreneurs ship faster.

F823 LLC is a software development and publishing company focused on creating enterprise-grade tools for modern businesses. Founded in 2025 and based in Marietta, Ohio, we're passionate about reducing the time and complexity of building great products.

Our Mission

To empower developers and entrepreneurs by providing production-ready tools and educational content that eliminate repetitive work and accelerate time to market. We believe in building once, using everywhere, and helping our customers focus on what makes their product unique instead of rebuilding foundational infrastructure.

What We Do

Software Development

We create enterprise-grade SaaS boilerplates and development tools that save teams months of development time. Our flagship product, SaaStastic, provides a complete B2B SaaS foundation with multi-tenant architecture, role-based access control, billing integration, and comprehensive testing.

Digital Publishing

We publish practical guides and workbooks for entrepreneurs, developers, and business owners. Our publications focus on actionable advice, step-by-step processes, and real-world applications that help readers achieve specific goals.

Educational Content

Through documentation, tutorials, and community support, we help developers learn best practices for building scalable, secure, and maintainable applications. We believe in teaching not just what works, but why it works.
